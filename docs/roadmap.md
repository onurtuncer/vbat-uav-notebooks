# Roadmap & Extensions

Planned extensions to the current model include:

## Near-Term
- Wing sizing and stall constraints
- EDF continuous vs burst power limits
- Disk loading trade studies

## Mid-Term
- Thermal modeling of motor and ESC
- Noise and downwash estimation
- Transition-phase power modeling

## Long-Term
- Hybrid-electric scaling
- Fuel-powered comparison
- Control authority and stability margins
